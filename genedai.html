Gene Dai currently focuses on user growth, platform products, and user experience design as the Co-founder of OpenJobs AI. He has served as product lead at multiple renowned internet companies, bringing extensive expertise in platform and tool product development.

He specializes in platform and tool product design, with deep domain knowledge across recruitment, fitness & wellness, e-commerce, and sharing economy verticals. From user acquisition and growth strategies to product roadmaps, he has hands-on experience scaling products from 0-to-1 launches through mature optimization cycles.

His career spans leadership roles including Platform Product Lead at Zuoyebang, User Product Lead at Liepin, Product Director at Smzdm, Community & Social Product Lead at Boss Zhipin, and Product & Business Lead at Kanzhun.

Additionally, he held product leadership positions at Dora and served as Product Partner & CPO at Feel, accumulating comprehensive product experience across both startups and large-scale platforms.

He's passionate about user experience design and excels at data-driven product decisions. In his spare time, he explores emerging product paradigms and business models, with a particular focus on user behavior analytics and growth optimization.
