Role Description

Data Scientists are analytical experts who utilize their skills in both technology and social science to find trends and manage data. They use industry knowledge, contextual understanding, skepticism of existing assumptions ‚Äì to uncover solutions to business challenges.

Key Responsibilities

  • Collect, process, and analyze large datasets to extract meaningful insights
  • Develop and implement machine learning models and statistical analyses
  • Create data visualizations and reports to communicate findings
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams to solve business problems
  • Design experiments and test hypotheses to drive data-driven decisions
  • Maintain and improve data quality and data infrastructure
