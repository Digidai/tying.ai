Core Marketing Concepts

4P Marketing Mix

Product, Price, Place, Promotion are the core framework of traditional marketing theory.

Customer Lifetime Value (CLV)

The net value a customer generates for a business over their entire relationship, a key metric for measuring customer importance.

Conversion Funnel

Describes the customer's conversion process from initial contact to final purchase, helping identify optimization opportunities.

Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)

The average cost to acquire a new customer, an important metric for evaluating marketing efficiency.

Brand Loyalty

The consumer's tendency to repeatedly purchase a specific brand and their emotional attachment to it.

Market Segmentation

Dividing a large market into smaller groups with similar needs or characteristics for precise marketing.

Marketing Automation

Using software platforms and technology to automate repetitive marketing tasks, improving efficiency and personalization.

Growth Hacking

A methodology for rapidly achieving user growth and business expansion through innovative, low-cost strategies.

Omnichannel Marketing

Providing a seamless, consistent customer experience across all marketing channels to achieve synergy.

User Persona

A virtual character model representing target user groups, built based on real data.

Marketing Funnel Optimization

A systematic approach to analyzing and improving conversion rates at each stage of the customer journey, from awareness to purchase.
